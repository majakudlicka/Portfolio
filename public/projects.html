BEEVR is an MVP (Minimum Viable Product) created for a Cambridge-based start-up. The app connects students- who need some extra cash - with local residents; who need help with various ad-hoc tasks. The MVP was built using modern React and Redux; making it easily scaleable for the potential further development of the app . We worked following agile methodology and mobile-first design. The app has received positive feedback from the Product Owner and the investors.
